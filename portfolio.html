This video showcases my design work for UNV Bangladesh’s International Volunteer Day, where I developed a cohesive visual identity spanning banners, stage backdrops, venue decorations, signage, award crests, certificates, presentation checks, and supporting print and digital communications materials. The designs elevated the event’s atmosphere and celebrated volunteers with a polished, impactful experience aligned with UNV’s global branding.
